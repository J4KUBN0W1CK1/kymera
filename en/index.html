What You May Specify

The original is born in dialogue.

The exact visual cannot be ordered — the final expression is always the artist's interpretation. What you provide is the framework within which the work comes into being.

01

Theme

Stillness, weight, strength, silence, contrast, touch… what expression should the sculpture carry?

02

Mechanical Element

A specific gear, piston, drive, or mechanism to be present within the work.

03

Posture

Standing figure, bust, head, seated, reclining, pair, dynamic scene.

04

Scale

Tabletop (40–60 cm), floor-standing (80–140 cm), or an object scaled to the space.

05

Finish

Metal, brass, patina, custom colour. Each layer transforms the character of the work.

06

Interpretation

From the brief, an original emerges — singular, irreproducible. Therein lies the value.

About

Human form.
Technical intervention.
Character of space.

Kymera arises from the tension between body and machine. Between silence and mechanism. Between what is alive, and what has been placed within the living.

The foundation of each sculpture is carved wood. Into it, cogwheels, pistons, drives, and further mechanical elements are surgically implanted. The entire work then receives its final surface — metal, brass, patina, or colour according to the collector's wishes.

  • Originals only — no editions, no reproductions.
  • Wood + technical elements + a finish tailored to each work.
  • Certificate of originality and individual numbering with every piece.
