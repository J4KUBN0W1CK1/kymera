Summary

kymera-art.com does not use cookies to track users. We do not install Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or any other marketing or analytical tracking. This website is intentionally simple and respects your privacy.

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored by your browser when you visit a website. They may serve various purposes — from the technical functioning of a site to tracking your behaviour across the internet. We do not engage in the latter.

Cookies used by kymera-art.com

The website currently sets no cookies of its own. Your browser may locally store minor technical information (e.g. form state) as part of standard modern browser behaviour — this is not tracking and does not require your consent.

Category Status Note
Technical cookies (necessary) 1 in use Cookie banner consent — see table below
Analytical cookies Not in use No Google Analytics or alternatives
Marketing / advertising cookies Not in use No Facebook Pixel, no remarketing
Personalisation cookies Not in use —

Typography — self-hosted fonts

The KYMERA website uses typefaces (Cormorant Garamond, Inter) hosted directly on its own server. They are therefore not loaded from Google Fonts or any other third-party service, and no visitor data is transmitted to any third party during page rendering.

Status

Fonts are served as local WOFF2 files from kymera-art.com — no contact with Google Fonts servers takes place.

What kymera-art.com does not do and will not do

  • Track your movements across the internet
  • Share data about you with advertising networks
  • Run retargeting campaigns based on your visit
  • Measure on-site behaviour via third-party tools
  • Require cookie consent, as no tracking cookies are used

Browser settings

You can manage cookies in general through your browser's settings. Information on managing cookies can be found in the documentation of your specific browser. Since kymera-art.com uses no tracking cookies, we have no need to offer a cookie consent bar or consent manager.
