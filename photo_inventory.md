## Notes on Visual Quality

- **Lighting:** Most showroom photos are taken in natural light from large windows. Some have window glare/reflections creating lens haze. Overall exposure is good.
- **Sharpness:** All inspected files appear sharp and detailed. Large file sizes (3–4.7 MB) suggest good original resolution.
- **Background complexity:** The showroom background is rich (sports cars, wheels, rally liveries) — usable as context photography or removable via masking. Cars add brand personality that aligns with KYMERA's automotive-art identity.
- **Angle variety:** The collection covers full-body shots, profile views, close-up head details, torso details, and back views across multiple sculptures.
- **For background removal:** Files with the sculpture against the large windows (natural light backlighting) will be easier to mask than those where the sculpture blends into darker interior elements.
